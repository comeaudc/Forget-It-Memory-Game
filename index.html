Forget About It!

This game was designed to test you noggin and your patience.

How To Play:

  1. Enter player names when prompted.
  2. Player located on lefthand side goes first.
  3. Click on 2 squares to reveal what is underneath.
  4. If they match, player gains 10 points and is allowed to choose again.
  5. If they do not match, next player goes.
  6. When all tiles are flipped player with the most points wins!
